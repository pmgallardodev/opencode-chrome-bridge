# Code of conduct

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Discuss technical decisions on their merits, make room for different levels of experience, and protect other people's privacy and security.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, publication of private information, and abusive language are not acceptable.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies to project repositories, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other spaces managed by the project. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and handled proportionately.
